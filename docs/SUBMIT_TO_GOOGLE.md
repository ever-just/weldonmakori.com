# Submit to Google Search Console - Final Steps

You've already verified ownership in Google Search Console. Now complete these steps to get indexed:

## Step 1: Submit Your Sitemap

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Select your property: `https://weldonmakori.com`
3. Click **Sitemaps** in the left sidebar
4. In the "Add a new sitemap" field, enter: `sitemap.xml`
5. Click **Submit**

✅ You should see "Success" status within a few seconds

## Step 2: Request Indexing for Homepage

1. In Google Search Console, click **URL Inspection** in the left sidebar
2. Enter: `https://weldonmakori.com`
3. Click **Enter**
4. Wait for Google to check the URL
5. Click **Request Indexing**
6. Wait 1-2 minutes for the request to process
7. Click **OK**

## Step 3: Request Indexing for Alternate Domain

Repeat Step 2 for:
- `https://www.weldonmakori.com`
- `https://weldon.lol` (if added as domain alias)
